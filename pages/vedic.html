Two Approaches to Problem Solving

Ancient wisdom meets modern technology

Vedic Mathematics

Ancient Calculation System

Vedic Mathematics is a collection of techniques derived from ancient Indian scriptures called the Vedas. These methods simplify arithmetic, algebraic, and geometric calculations.

Example: Multiplication of Numbers Close to 100

To multiply 98 × 97:

1. Calculate differences from 100: 98-100 = -2, 97-100 = -3

2. Cross add: 98 + (-3) = 95 or 97 + (-2) = 95

3. Multiply differences: (-2) × (-3) = 06

4. Result: 9506

Advantages

  • Mental calculation capabilities
  • Improves number sense and flexibility
  • No external tools required
  • Enhances logical thinking

Artificial Intelligence

Modern Computational Power

AI uses machine learning algorithms and neural networks to process complex calculations, recognize patterns, and solve problems at incredible speeds.

Example: AI-Powered Calculation

To multiply 98 × 97:

1. AI can instantly recognize this as a calculation task

2. Process through neural networks trained on arithmetic

3. Generate result: 9506

4. Can also explain steps or solve much more complex problems

Advantages

  • Extremely fast processing
  • Handles complex, multi-step problems
  • Can learn and improve over time
  • Integrates with various data sources
